The Case Study

The Loyalty Engine

This section breaks down the core business challenge we faced and the strategic goals I established for the Loyalty Engine product to address these issues head-on.

The Problem

The business was facing a significant challenge with **high customer churn** and **low transaction frequency** within key user segments. This indicated a lack of long-term engagement and value perception among our users.

The Goal (My North Star)

My primary objective was to reverse these trends. I set a clear goal: **Increase Active Loyal Users and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** within 12 months by creating a compelling, sticky product experience.

Strategic Architecture: The Loyalty Flywheel
